MAZE GAME

Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to navigate the maze. Press "H" to get a hint as to the next cell you should visit. Press "P" to display the shortest path to the maze exit. Your score will go up by 5 points if you visit the next closest cell on the shortest path, and go down by 3 if you go to a cell not on the shortest path.
